Fireside with Shereef

08/31/2014

After watching the Fireside Chat with Shereef, I was both inspired and challenged.

I'm incredibly excited to become a mad scientist. But I prefer the term wizard. Either way--I loved what he said about not expecting the experience to be handed to you--it isn't as simple as, "Here's the syllabus--do these things, get a grade, get a job". It's about work--pushing, grinding, getting farther every day and improving yourself.

I'm not so excited about saying "I don't know." But that's part of the process, right? So I'd better get used to it.

I'm worried about the schedule--because I'm afraid I'll push myself too hard and risk burning out. The sleep schedule of DBC Chicago already freaks me out--that's what spending a year self-employed will do to you.

Either way, I'm excited to get in there. But, for now, I'm going to enjoy the fact that I finished this week's work an hour before midnight and go watch some Orange is the New Black.
